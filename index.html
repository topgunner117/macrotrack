API keys
Get a free Gemini key at aistudio.google.com/apikey and (optional) a USDA key at fdc.nal.usda.gov/api-key-signup. Everything is stored only on this device (localStorage). Since your key lives in the browser, keep this app to yourself.
Sync across devices
Enter the same sync key on another device (or open ?sync=YOURKEY) to load your log there. Your food log & targets sync automatically; your API keys stay on each device. Keep the key private — anyone with it can read your log.
